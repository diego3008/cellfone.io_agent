================================================================================
CELLFONE.IO – CUSTOMER POLICIES & GUARANTEES
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. WARRANTY POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.1 New Products
All new Apple products (iPhones, AirPods, Apple Watch, MacBooks) sold at
Cellfone.io come with the full manufacturer's limited warranty provided by Apple.
This warranty covers hardware defects and manufacturing faults from the date of
purchase. Customers are encouraged to register their product directly with Apple
to activate the warranty.

1.2 Open-Box Products
All open-box products sold at Cellfone.io come with a 6-month Cellfone.io
limited warranty. This warranty covers:
  - Hardware defects not caused by physical damage or misuse.
  - Failure of internal components under normal usage conditions.

The open-box warranty does NOT cover:
  - Accidental damage (cracked screens, water damage, drops).
  - Damage caused by unauthorized repairs or modifications.
  - Normal wear and tear (battery degradation, cosmetic scratches).

1.3 Extended Warranty (CellProtect Plan)
Customers may purchase the CellProtect Plan at checkout to extend coverage:
  - Open-Box products: extends warranty from 6 months to 24 months.
  - New products: adds 12 months of coverage on top of Apple's manufacturer warranty.
The CellProtect Plan also includes one free screen replacement per year.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Return Window
  - New products: 30-day return window from the date of delivery.
  - Open-box products: 15-day return window from the date of delivery.

2.2 Eligibility for Returns
To be eligible for a return, the product must:
  - Be in its original condition (no new physical damage introduced by the customer).
  - Include all original accessories and packaging.
  - Be accompanied by the original order confirmation or receipt.

2.3 Non-Returnable Items
  - Products that have been activated and used for more than 5 days after delivery
    are subject to a 15% restocking fee.
  - AirPods and Apple Watch bands that have been opened are non-returnable for
    hygiene reasons unless they are defective.
  - Products purchased under a final-sale or clearance promotion cannot be returned.

2.4 How to Initiate a Return
Customers must contact Cellfone.io support at support@cellfone.io or via the
in-app chat to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number before
shipping any product back. Returns without a valid RMA number will not be accepted.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. REFUND POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1 Approved Refunds
Once a returned item is received and inspected (within 3–5 business days),
Cellfone.io will notify the customer of the refund approval status.

3.2 Refund Methods
  - Credit/Debit Card payments: Refunded to the original card within 5–10
    business days after approval.
  - Financing/Installment plans: The outstanding balance is cancelled and any
    payments already made are refunded within 7–14 business days.
  - Store credit: Customers may opt for store credit instead of a monetary refund,
    which is processed within 24 hours of approval.

3.3 Partial Refunds
A partial refund may be issued when:
  - The product shows signs of use beyond inspection.
  - Accessories or original packaging are missing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. INSTALLMENT & FINANCING POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 12-Month Installment Plan
Cellfone.io offers a 12-month installment plan on all products. Monthly payments
are calculated at the time of purchase and displayed on the product listing.
  - A soft credit check is performed at checkout; this does not affect the
    customer's credit score.
  - Payments are automatically charged to the customer's payment method on the
    same date each month.

4.2 Early Payoff
Customers may pay off the remaining installment balance at any time with no
early-payoff penalty or additional fees.

4.3 Late Payments
  - A grace period of 5 calendar days is granted after the payment due date.
  - After the grace period, a late fee of $15 is applied.
  - Accounts with two or more consecutive missed payments may have their
    installment plan suspended and the remaining balance made due immediately.

4.4 Cancellation of Financing
If a financed product is returned within the eligible return window, the
installment plan is cancelled and any payments made are refunded as per Section 3.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. SHIPPING POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 Processing Time
Orders are processed and shipped within 1–2 business days of purchase
confirmation. Open-box orders may require an additional 1 business day for
quality inspection before shipment.

5.2 Shipping Options
  - Standard Shipping (5–7 business days): Free on all orders over $100.
  - Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days): $15 flat fee.
  - Overnight Shipping (next business day): $35 flat fee.

5.3 Shipping Carriers
Cellfone.io ships via UPS, FedEx, or USPS depending on destination and availability.
Tracking information is emailed to the customer once the order is shipped.

5.4 International Shipping
At this time, Cellfone.io only ships within the contiguous United States.
Hawaii, Alaska, and US territories may be subject to additional shipping fees
and longer delivery times.

5.5 Lost or Damaged in Transit
If a package is lost or arrives damaged:
  - Customers must report the issue within 48 hours of the expected delivery date.
  - Cellfone.io will file a carrier claim and either ship a replacement unit or
    issue a full refund within 5–7 business days of the claim being filed.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. OPEN-BOX PRODUCT GRADING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cellfone.io uses a three-tier grading system for all open-box products:

  Grade A – Like New
    The device shows no visible signs of use. Screen and body are free of
    scratches or scuffs. Battery health is 90% or above.

  Grade B – Good
    The device may have minor cosmetic imperfections (light scratches on the body)
    that are not visible when the screen is on. Battery health is 80–89%.

  Grade C – Fair
    The device has visible scratches or scuffs on the body or screen bezel.
    Fully functional. Battery health is 70–79%.

All open-box products are factory reset, professionally cleaned, and tested
against a 72-point quality checklist before being listed for sale.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. PRIVACY & DATA POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 Device Data Wipe
All open-box devices are fully wiped and restored to factory settings before sale.
Cellfone.io is not responsible for any data left on devices by the original owner
if the device was traded in. Customers are advised to erase their own devices
before trade-in.

7.2 Customer Data
Personal information collected during checkout (name, address, payment details)
is used solely for order processing and is never sold to third parties. Full
details are available in the Cellfone.io Privacy Policy at cellfone.io/privacy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. TRADE-IN POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8.1 Trade-In Eligibility
Cellfone.io accepts trade-ins for Apple iPhones, iPads, and MacBooks. Devices
must be:
  - Fully functional (powers on, touchscreen works, no cracked screen).
  - Not reported as lost or stolen (checked against industry databases).
  - Unlocked or carrier-unlocked.

8.2 Trade-In Value
Trade-in quotes are valid for 14 days. The final trade-in value is confirmed
after Cellfone.io receives and inspects the device. If the device's condition
does not match the customer's self-reported condition, an adjusted quote will
be offered. Customers may decline the adjusted quote and have their device
returned at no charge.

8.3 Trade-In Credit
Trade-in value is applied as a credit toward the purchase price. It cannot be
redeemed as cash.
